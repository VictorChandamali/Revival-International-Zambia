About Revival Hub

Raising a Generation Rooted in Prayer and Truth

Revival Hub Zambia is a Christ-centered ministry committed to building believers with a strong altar life and a deep relationship with God.

Through teachings, gatherings, and discipleship, we equip individuals to walk in purpose, live transformed lives, and influence their world through Christ.

We believe that every generation has a divine mandate — and that mandate is unlocked through prayer, the Word, and intentional community.

Revival Hub Zambia exists to be a furnace where those flames are ignited, sustained, and sent out to the nations.

Our gatherings are more than meetings — they are encounters that redefine who you are in God.
